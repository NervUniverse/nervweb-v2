Our History

In the early months of 2023, a visionary gamer with a deep-rooted desire to establish a thriving and inclusive gaming community embarked on a remarkable journey. On the auspicious day of March 20th, this passionate individual laid the foundation for a remarkable venture. Fueled by their unwavering love for gaming and a fervent aspiration to create a space where all gamers would feel welcome, they set out to bring their vision to life.

Over the subsequent months, this fledgling community began to take shape, evolving into a vibrant hub where players from all walks of life could come together, connect, and revel in their shared passion. Guided by the founder's unwavering dedication, the community flourished, attracting an eclectic mix of participants who were united by their unwavering enthusiasm for gaming.
